## Common questions

**How do I delete my account?**
Open Becom → Profile → Privacy & Security → Delete Account.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
iOS Settings → your Apple Account → Subscriptions → Becom → Cancel.

**My subscription isn't working / I was charged but don't see Pro features**
Open Becom → Profile → Restore Purchases. If that doesn't fix it, email us.

**How do I reset my progress?**
Email us — we'll help you out.
